About Wazor

In 2007, Wazor revolutionised the way women styled their hair with the launch of its first styler – a professional tool designed for salon use that could also be easily used at home. Wazor has continued to be a leader and innovator in hair styling with its wide range of professional stylers , hair dryers, hair product and hair brushes.
